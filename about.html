what is plum?
plum is a small project by some computer science freshmen of the university FAU Erlangen of 2013.
being a student project, plum is supposed to give you the possibility of telling other students at which structure you're currently at.
you soon™ will be able to publish your current seat in a lecture. -nakami

developers of plum: nakami, ra1n, cerox, mastela, MrNugget, SpitfireX, Witty
